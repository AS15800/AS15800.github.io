About us

The Islamic Society Greenwich is committed to provide everyone with engaging and relevant activities.
The Isoc welcomes all students and members of the university, Muslims, people of different faith and no faith alike, aiming to understand Islam and live by it's teachings and principles.

We believe that working for Islam is not only about campaigning for Muslim rights, but also about the rights of others, about uniting with others for the common good.
