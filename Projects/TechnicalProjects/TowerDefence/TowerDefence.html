SALAH 101

A lightweight application (under 5 MB) that shows how to pray according to the Qur’an and Authentic Hadith. It is built by reverts who went through the challenges of learning to pray to enable people to learn to pray quickly.

CONTENT

All the contents are written and are kept in-line according to the Qur’an and Sunnah. All the contents are carefully written and checked before uploading. If you notice any mistake, please do let us know.

FUTURE

We have plans to continue updating this app with new and more content so you know everything about prayer and Wudu.

SUPPORT

As this application is free, we want to make sure it is available to as many people as possible. We also don’t have any ads as we can’t have full control over the ads that are shown. To continue updating, maintaining and supporting our mission to teach Islam, we require support, telling people about the app and financial support. We have opened a patreon page where people are able to donate and support our mission.
